Welcome to Frawlsalla

Frawlsalla is a free to play 2D platform fighting game on PS5, PS4, Xbox, and mobile devices.

Try to knock other players out and be the winner of Frawlsalla!

Choose your fighter and weapon suitable for you, then prove your skills into the arena!

Features:

  • Online Ranked 1v1

  • Online Ranked 2v2

  • 4 Player Online Free for All

  • Cross-play Custom Rooms

  • Local Single Player

  • Training Room
